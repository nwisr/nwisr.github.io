Contact Eric

NWISR is operated by Eric Beck, the United States Adult Soccer Association Region IV Referee Coordinator. Eric Beck is known for providing referee assignment services to leagues and tournaments as well as running the Metro PDX 7v7 soccer league. He has been involved in the soccer arena for over 50 years and was inducted into the USASA National Soccer Hall-of-Fame in 2010.

Eric is available by phone between the hours of 8AM and 4PM at 503-662-8182. Correspondence may be mailed to 2169 SE Scott Avenue, Gresham OR 97080
